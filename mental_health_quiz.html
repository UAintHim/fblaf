Mental Health Quiz

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed with school or activities?

2. How well do you understand your own emotions when you feel stressed or upset?

3. How often do you use healthy coping habits (exercise, hobbies, breaks, talking to someone)?

4. How confident are you in handling stressful situations?

5. Do you feel like you have at least one person you can talk to when you’re stressed?

6. How stressed do school assignments or deadlines make you feel?

7. How often do you feel pressure to be “perfect”?

8. How often do you do activities that make you happy or help you relax?
